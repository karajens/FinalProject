Experience

My experience in marketing up to this point.

My marketing experience includes two years as part of the marketing team at BoardCo Boats, where I’ve contributed to a wide range of projects, from running targeted ad campaigns to tackling design-focused initiatives and website overhauls. Operating within the boating industry—a highly niche market—has presented unique challenges in reaching the right audience. This has required me to think creatively and develop innovative strategies to connect with potential customers effectively.

During this time, I successfully built three websites designed to expand our audience and connect with more potential customers, and I also helped restructure a fourth. These projects allowed me to gain valuable insights into user engagement and online optimization. What I’ve enjoyed most is analyzing what attracts customers and experimenting with outside-the-box methods to improve profitability, blending creativity with data-driven decision-making to deliver impactful results.

My favorite work I've done with the guidance of my marketing director would be the BoardCo Boats site. This site is updated frequently, but only undergoes complete a complete rebranding once in a while. I was able to give a lot of input as to what this site should look and feel like for our customers.
